About

I'm a music producer, pianist, drummer and singer based in LA, California. My style's heavily influenced by the 70's and 80's with a good dose of electronic dance music.

I've been producing for a long time now, but only recently pursuing things seriously. Over the years, I've made dozens of half finished songs that I'd share only with close friends. One of them said, "You've gotta put these out there man! You should finish them and share them with the world".

So I'm following his advice. The first release was "Lover", which you can listen to here.

Stay tuned, I have much more coming.

- Benja

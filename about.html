About

Hey guys, this is Benja aka Benja

I'm an electronic music producer based in LA, California. I'm not tied to a specific genre, but if it makes you move - physically or emotionally - then I've done my job. I love using synthesizers (especially my Prophet Rev 2 if you haven't noticed), but love using organic sounds I'm your new favorite music artist. I write my own music, produce it and as of recent been mixing and mastering it as well.

My main DAW is Ableton Live 10, though I may use Logic Pro X for tracking and comping.

My hardware:

  • Universal Audio Apollo x8 Audio Interface
  • Universal Audio 1176LN Compressor
  • Chandler Limited TG2 Preamp
  • SSL xLogic G Series Stereobus Compressor
  • Dave Instruments Prophet Rev2
  • Roland TR8s
  • Yamaha HS8 Monitors
